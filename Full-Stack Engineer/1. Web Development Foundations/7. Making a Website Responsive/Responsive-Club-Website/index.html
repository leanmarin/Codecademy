Exploring nature in Argentina since 1998

At Mountain Hikers we are passionate about nature and we love exploring the mountains of Argentina. Founded in 1998 by a group of adventurous friends, our club has become a community of hiking and mountaineering lovers.

Mission

Promote the practice of hiking and mountaineering in a safe and sustainable manner, encouraging respect and conservation of the environment and local culture.

Vision

To be a reference in the promotion of hiking and mountaineering in Argentina, standing out for our ethics and responsibility in the practice of these activities and contributing to the sustainable development of mountain areas.

Our club offers various activities for our members, from excursions at different levels of difficulty to orienteering workshops, camping and lectures on mountaineering related topics. We also organize expeditions to other places in Argentina and the world.

About Mountain Hikers

Mountain Hikers is a community of nature lovers who share a passion for outdoor adventures. Our mission is to promote hiking and trekking as a healthy and sustainable way of exploring the natural world.

Founded in 1998 in the beautiful city of Bariloche, Argentina, Mountain Hikers has grown into a vibrant community of like-minded individuals who come together to organize hikes, workshops, and social events. Our members range from experienced mountaineers to beginners who are just starting to explore the joys of hiking.
